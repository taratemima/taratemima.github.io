Consolation Prize
I fail to understand
How you break people
Fragile and fair
As mirrors

With a bully's rationale:
“It isn't my problem
He was in my way.
Not responsible for his feelings,
He was in my way.”

I keep my mouth shut
Protecting my dreams from you,
Watch as you get the sweet things
You donut deserve.

He didn't deserve your lies.
You're nothing under that mask.

Wading through peoples lives,
Leaving wreckage and singing along,
Depending on lies.
Looking down on all of us
Foolish enough to trust.

When you remember
All the night terrors you had,
No one will hold you
When you're empty and sad.

When you exit,
Losing favor in loving eyes,
Enjoy being above it all,
Enjoy your consolation prize.

Game II
Its a carnival game to you
My infatuation like the others
A brass ring to win.

Eyes can distinguish now
Between a loving voice and childish hopes.

She dumped, she said
“Its a carnival mask
You'll never see my face.”

Eyes can distinguish now
Between lies and guiding light.

I'm not going to hold this grudge.
Let her play her limited game.
Efforts to heal are sufficient,
Efforts toward perception are sufficient.

I win.

Collide
The glass cities I built
Reflect the light from outside.
The gates are open,
I see light collide.

I am finally joyous and eager
As an adult.
I take in the kaleidoscope universe
Green nature reflected in green eyes.

Reality is a child's dream
Of color and warmth,
Rose, bronze, jade and indigo--
I see light collide.

Night Terrors and Madness
Reality is the face behind the madness.
How many of us weren't carefree
And never will be?

No comfort, indifference.
Such treatment stunts the methods behind the madness.
Such treatment stunts the face behind,
I'm restraining myself from good.

And I just donut know if I could accept my fate,
To keep our fate, our dreams inside, be they light or dark.

I could just accept our fate, to keep our finished adult lives.
We cannot laugh and tell lies within ourselves.
When others tell me to keep doubts and anguish quiet,
I dare reveal the victim, I dare bare the confusion.

Night terrors wake us, screaming, alone and yes,
This lengthy drawn-out complaint-fest is mine,
My perception, and I will never keep quiet again.

Theory of Mind
You never give up your search for God, do you, girl?
‘Secret’ and ‘unknown’ are trigger words.
The need to map out my inner life
Like Darwin on the islands.

I donut care if you think
My inner light is swamp gas.
Where I am,
So much light you'd be blind,
So much depth you'd be lost. 
