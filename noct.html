It's a night of fire
a night of buildings made ash.
Third degree burns on a four year old,
mother in a body bag.

Emmett dresses the wounds
while I radio the hospital
and sing to myself and the child.

While everyone is asleep
I ride in an ambulance
I walk by the moon
while others walk by the sun.
They dream of peace
while I try to relieve pain--
reverse circidian,
nocturnal.

Christ, where the hell are you
why I am such an outcast?
I may be fat and talk so slow
but my hands and mind are quick.

As I take another cup of coffee
and clean up the bed
I sing to myself and close my eyes

While everyone is asleep
I ride in an ambulance
I walk by the moon
while others walk by the sun.
They dream of peace
while I try to relieve pain--
reverse circidian,
nocturnal.
