Ballad of a Green Pen

[winter, 1997]

I had a nightmare
where I was awake, getting dressed,
and before I knew it,
was struggling to take notes.
It was no dream.

Oh head pound, pound, pound
like a welder.
Oh eyes burn, burn, burn
like a blowtorch to steel.

I'm falling in love
with my green pen
as it twirls on the paper.
Leaving its mark
in forms of trees and cocks.
It's the color of traffic lights and Astroturf
a green brighter than
in grass or trees.
It ran and skipped over blue lines
even beyond it--
this pen is the only thing that hasn't failed me.
