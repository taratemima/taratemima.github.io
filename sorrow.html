To Learn Sorrow

Self-pity is easy
when you lose your innocence.

You must think you're the first
to be cut, betrayed, and
forsaken.

Your suffering is not the only suffering.
Even the cop, even the teacher, even
the parents you leech from and complain about
have their pains--
secret and otherwise.

Don't hate them.
Don't hate yourself.

Learn compassion--
and don't go quoting the _Satanic Bible_ at me.

It's not going to help you
here in your inner grey.

It's not depression that makes you "deep"--
it's seeing pain,
and wishing at least,
to soothe yours and your loved ones
and to see no-one else suffers.

This is the light in your inner darkness--
this is the insight introspection gives.

And if you don't take my advice?
I don't know--
your cycle of self-pity and hatred
and blind following
can run you ragged.

I won't be there to pick you up.
