Grey Autumn

a short song cycle
 

Autumn Comes In


Watch this pomergarante sunset
Shine on cobwebbed pine.
Violins usher in
A colder day.
Swirling leaves around my doorstep,
The days grow shorter,
the shadows grow longer.

Pull out this plum jacket,
Bundle myself in.
White noise ushers in
A sparkling night.
Spiders spin in my corner,
The distance grows longer,
The debts pile higher.

I need to find something
That survives the seasons
Before I die.
 

Sunday


The heat got shut off
On cold, rainy Sunday.
Dead leaves get stuck in gutters,
sucked in.
The paint falls off,
But I'm holding to these four walls,
Holding to these four walls.

My only friends are the spiders in the corner.
They're the only ones I'm not too broke to see.
Orbs and domes all over the ceiling
In a gray room,
Grayer than outside.

There's no gold in autumn,
No cream for me.
Shades of sky and mud clog the room
Like gutters.
Full of dust and cobwebs,
But I'm holding to these four walls,
Holding to these four walls.

My only friends are the spiders in the corner.
They're the only ones I'm not too broke to see.
Orbs and domes all over the ceiling
In a gray room,
Grayer than outside.

Things are falling apart
But I hold on
Like a spider.
 

The Last Rose of Summer


Your eyes
Like dark pools
Full with fallen leaves.

I cry
Must pay my debts,
Must move away from you.

So I give the last rose of summer
and the first fallen leaf of autumn.
I will come back for you another season,
I will come for you someday soon.

This rose
Color of sunshine,
Glow like candle in flame.

This leaf,
Color of brass,
Curl like a censer.

So I give the last rose of summer
And the first fallen leaf of autumn.
I will come back for you another season,
I will come for you someday soon.

Once I said I'd never fall in love
Because people never love me back.
But when someone does love me back,
I can't stay for long
Because of my mistakes.

So I give the last rose of summer
And the first fallen leaf of autumn.
I will come back for you another season,
I will come for you someday soon.
 

New Years


It's the same year on repeat for me,
A few Halloween parties,
A few Rosh Hashana apples,
Can't fast
When no one cares if I do
There's nothing for me here.
There's no new year for me here.

I can't explain the distance
between me and others.
I can't explain why.
I either believe in
too much or
believe in nothing.

I make the same mistakes
While the time grows shorter,
Where's my new year?

I fear whispers,
No one could accept me,
Where's my new year?

I can't explain,
why I am not casting my lot.
I just wonder
where's my new year?

Where's my New Year? 
