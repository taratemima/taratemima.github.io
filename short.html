I Betrayed

 I waited my life for a denoument,
a perfectly executed "fuck you",
a perfect revenge for a lifetime of pain.

 But in this quest,
this feverish denouncement,
I hurt you.
I did it,
I am another person who hurt you,
and unlike *my* tormenters,
I am tortured with that knowledge.

 Machine

 The beat of my heart
and the hum of the machine.
I should go to bed, but
honestly I'm seduced
by the stars, the silence,
and the promise of inspiration
under flourscent lights.
I feel like the Milky Way
is revolving inside me,
that I am the stars.

 I should go to bed . . .

Just a Shattered Notion

Summer lies.
Summer nights lie even more.
It tells of sweethearts,
in the sunshine, in the moonlight,
meeting, talking, doing more.
"This could be you too," it whispers.
But all that happens to you during the day
is feeling's slow decay.
All that you do in the night
is cry your eyes out and curse the short night.
