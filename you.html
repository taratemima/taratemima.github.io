A Song of You

Every song
about tears and kisses
every song
addressed to a 'you'
takes form
in an address to you
even after all this time.

Why does every song have to be about you?
Why does the 'you' have your hair and smile?
Why does the 'you' have your compassion
and your callousness?

Ten years ago I met you
when I was young and stupid
so you made your mark--
I can never trust anyone else
I can never love anyone else.
Gloating yet?

Why does every song have to be about you?
Why does the 'you' have your hair and smile?
Why does the 'you' have your compassion
and your callousness?
