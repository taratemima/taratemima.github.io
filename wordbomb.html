Wordbomb

I want to make a bomb
not of nitrogylcerine
but of sharp accusations,
nasty curses,
low blows.

My rage will be the fuel
my confusion will the spark
and you and your arrogance
will be the catalyst.

I want to make a bomb
that doesn't break your mailbox
or crash your system
but that sends your neat world view
(where I am considered so little)
into oblivion.
