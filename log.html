More screaming & yelling

Last updated 9/29/98

If Jesus is the answer, then what was the question?"--Jeffrey Jay Lowder

"Talk into the hand!"--whenever Pastor Corbain gets in a person's face screaming random Bible cut-up, some would put up their palm, instructing Corbain to "talk to the hand." Some give helpful hints on giving up caffeine and taking up breath fresheners and Prozac.

Can I get a witness?--Once, a guy yelled "I don't know what that guy is saying, but I thank the Lord that I'm saved. I was on drugs, I was a wreak, then I accepted Jesus as my Lord and I was saved." You can be serious or sarcastic ("God cured a big mole on my ass").One thing I saw was dueling and defending preachers. A guy in a cheap maroon suit said such thing as "It doesn't have to proven, it just has to be believed," and a Christian yuppie. Just to prove not all Christians think alike, a kid with a ponytail kept calling Pastor Corbin "preacher of the law" and "Pharisee." An old guy once took out a pocket Bible and argued with Corbin. But let's not forget the "Sign Guy" (who just doesn't have the lungs for preaching), a Jehovah's Witness, a fundie-turned-agnostic, and a red-headed tract hander who argued furiously with Case

You shall take no other gods than me--Many have done just that. Some have invoked Eris, "Bob", gods of Asgard, the Teletubbies and Howard Stern. Pagans have sang hymns in praise of the Goddess (inculding an alternate version of "Amazing Grace"). And then there was this guy who dry humped a dog and yelled "Satan! Fuck me like the whore I am!" People have also attempted to make converts to the Church of the Happy Fuzzy Bunny (people have prostated themselves to him), the Church of the Great Pumpkin, and the Church of Wyatt ("all problems can be solved by oral sex"). People also have gotten involved in devil-worship--okay worship of a balloon vendor dressed as the devil.

Hit me!--I'd like to go on record and say I don't endorse physical violence. It doesn't mean others haven't tried it. People have tried to knock his glasses and hat. Some have yelled "Mosh pit!!!" One guy even kissed him. It can be funny--I don't endorse it-- really! Once, however, a fight broke out between two people--not fun. Another thing I wouldn't endorse is tearing up a Bible and eating (which was what some kids did). Put it to good use--quote contradicting verses at him. Another thing--if you have certain views on diet, beating people up will only make your case faulty. Just ask people who have to deal with some violent vegans.

Songfest--"A Beautiful Day in the Neighborhood," "Henry the Eighth," "Liar," "Heresy ("God is dead/and no one cares . . .")", "Yodelling Satan" and other such songs provide entertainment for all. There were some kids who tried to sing cheery songs about Jesus, but a little loudly sung Trent Reznor usually scares them off.

Talk back!--Some take different words as cues. The most popular is jumping up and down whenever Corbin does, and yelling along with his catchphrase "Open up your Bibles you heathens!" (some have replied "Heathens don't have Bibles!") When he says "sodomy," some hooted and cheer. When he says "fornication," people pretended they're having sex (like a young woman who laid on the street as her boyfriend pretended to have sex with her) or simply hoot and holler. And of course, "What about masturbation" is a popular question. Once, when he started using 'r' words (redemption, repentence, reconcillation, etc), we added a few of our own--'redundant,' 'repetitive,' and 'retard.' When he says "truth," Case yells "You want the truth? You can't handle the truth!" Neef also had said "You want vermouth? You can't handle vermouth!" When he says "law," Case yells "I am the law! I am Judge Dredd!" When the Pastor says "salvation," Case yells "Resistance is futile, salvation is irrevelent, you WILL become one with the Borg!" When the Pastor looks like he's pooped, people yell "Your powers are weak, old man." When Pastor says "Listen friends," I yell "You're not my friend! I have real friends!," "Stop calling me Fred!" "What friends?" or "You *have* no friends!" Of course, there's Case's act of going around Pastor Corbin with a toy tricorder and screaming "Jim! Jim! He has no brain!" There is also the act of twisting his words--"What? Lazarus was a ZOMBIE!" Lately people have also been quoting David Tibet and "South Park" ("Don't say 'pigfucker in front of Jesus!") Some guy also has yelled at Corbin "How could you call me friend after what we shared together! Gary, I'm having your baby!" Not like Gary hasn't gotten any good quotations. At one point he yelled "Get your ears out of your wax!"

"The most recent preacher was a pretty good one. Corbin made that "Get your ears out of your wax" blunder yet again, about the fifth time in my memory, and Warren came back with, "Have you got the nose out of your boogers? Have you got the ass out of your shit?" There were also at least two random men in brassieres, what's the chance of that in any week."

Signs--Jeff Chase sometimes puts up signs like "Atheists Do It With Style," "It All Started When Janet Reno Burned Down His Church," "Heathens Have More Fun," "Orgasms = Exist -- God = Doesn't," "Jesus of the Borg: Resistance Is Futile--You Will Be Asslimiated!," "Fire Is Cool Yeah Heh Heh You're All Going Into the Fire, Yeah Heh Heh Heh Heh" and "Pastor Corbin Is Feeling a Little Agitated Today." Other exchanges have revealed him to be a big old hypocrite:

CORBIN: If thou want to be perfect, go, sell your possessions, give the money to the poor, and follow me.

ME: Have you? Have you sold all you own and given the money to the poor?

CORBIN: [dead silence]

I did get a smile from him when I asked him if he was going to the Fetus Barbaque held by the Church of Euthensia.

Personae Dramatis--One guy, Garry, after being pissed that the Pastor was so persumptous to define his religion (Judaism) and say that he is going to hell (he's gay), that he took the persona of a Nazi jerk that yells that people should listen to the Pastor because "he's white, he's male, he's Protestant." His act came to an end when some kids followed him, thinking that he really was a Nazi. He is then tried to get the Pastor to marry him and his SO. A gender fucker in a purple dress, grey hat, and a "Jesus" name tag also made an apperance. Other pretenders to the Jesus throne have come around, including one offering $5 a fuck. And then there was these two guys and two girls making out in front of Corbin, as well as a girl flashing her breasts. Oh yeah, he really got through to them. There was also some guy going around talking about someone wearing a cape being "a member of the coven" and ranting about the apocolypse and the Marines. Some "Rocky rats (fans of late-night "Rocky Horror Picture Show" showings)" in drag yelled stuff at him, as did kids in black and Subgenii. Of course, Corbain can yell so loud that people get killed every night.

Free Debate--There are also those who wonder where the Pastor gets off. One guy argued that how could he say what the Bible is about since he doesn't know the orginial languages of the Bible. Some have wondered why people are so "mean" or "close-minded" when dealing with the Pastor. A normal-looking woman kept asking the Pastor if she can give her fiance a blowjob.

Weather is (Almost) Irrelevent to the Pastor--Snow, rain, and cold (and some conferenece in Tennessee) kept Pastor away for a while. But he was back in 0 or so degree weather screaming his lungs out and jumping and squating. That lead to this exchange.

Pastor: Do you want to go to hell?

Chuck: Sure, it's warm, and has cool people there.

It Just Gets Weirder All the Time--As if the Pastor was not entertainment enough, the Harvard wrestling time went through its initation. We were greeted by the site of men in leotards singing "Over the Rainbow" and dancing bad ballet. We saw men carrying signs that said "Practice Safe Sex Or Die" and "I'm Lebanese And I Love It." (instructs young boys to say "Hey hey/ho ho/Western imperialism/has got to go.") We saw Marty Hannigan's roomate and MIT alumnus Mike chant "Harvard eats its young!" Some also held a whipping line after Corbin left. Sign Guy dressed up as the Michelin Angel and to quote him, "I've recorded all the stupid things you said." Kinda like the log here.

Flawless Victory!--A man in a wheelchair found one way to get the Pastor running--scream real loud. For while, it looked like the Pastor was leaving, but he stays in the end. He always does. Never mind that he almost got arrested for distrubing the peace (must be a rookie). He just got on his knees, asking god to help him finish preaching. One wonders if Jesus is saying "Leave me alone, Gary."

Damn sodomites!--Of course, some women go up to the Pastor's daughters and say "Why don't we go out for coffee after this and talk about godly things?" Of course, the Pastor gets real uncomfortable at any mention at same sex fooling around, screaming some nonsense about 'sodomites' when the subject comes up. But it isn't all fun and games. When Pastor Corbin gave the usual 'AIDS is Gawd's punishment against the ho-mo-sex-you-alls' spiel, someone yelled at him that her best friend, a devout Christian, died of AIDS.
