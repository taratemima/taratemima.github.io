The people at CERN wanted to make information from other departments, other fields, and other lands as easy to get as a book from one branch of a public library to another.

The World Wide Web is like a library all right--one where diaries and Hustler issues are stacked with Ph.D theses. And I like it that way. Thus, the design idea.
