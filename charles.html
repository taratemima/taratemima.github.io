The scum always rises to the top.
A blanket of dirt hides this world
as I breast stroke through the dirty water
with my trusty snorkel.

I am in a beer-colored world,
scanvanging through the mottled sand.
My kicking feet reveals
decades-old costume jewelry and tires.

I'll tell you what I saw,
before I came out of the poisoned wonderland.

A hypodermic needle
flat against the sand,
like fossil in amber.
