Synthesis Darling

A strawberry moon
over cerulan seas,
gold charmeuse,
snow on Mount Carmel,
a database of unlimited knowledge and gigabytes,
a fine manifesto from an alternate dimension,
a klezmer song to wake the dead
a merciful djinn,
a perfect pink rosebud.

These are things
that I can't have,
and she,
who resembles and exceeds all them,
I can never have.
