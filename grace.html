Morning Grace

Why are you more attractive while dancing?
Thin twisted walking stick turning,
Black lace shirt that made skin look
like light filtered out of curtains.

I stare out the window wondering
On the train, fighting sleep,
where I can find the words to tell you and all
finally, I accept defeat at sleep's soft hands.

I wake up into a nightmare--
I would never kiss or touch or talk to you
or anyone I longed for ever:
the green haired girl,
the boy with razorblades,
the girl that kissed me at party
the boy that made out with me
(and it felt like fireworks)
the girl who hugged and kissed me in line for the bathroom
the couple from Waltham
you dancing on the floor.

I walked down to the kitchen hungry,
but I can't run across the Charles to sate it.
